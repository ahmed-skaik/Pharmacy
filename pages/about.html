About Us.

Al Azhar University has the privilege to establish the first Faculty of Pharmacy in Palestine in 1992. The Faculty of Pharmacy at Al Azhar University-Gaza (AUG) is considered one of the leading pharmaceutical education centers dedicated to preparing pharmacy professionals and scholars in Palestine. The Faculty has the most qualified staff in pharmaceutical education, who graduated from prestigious universities and who have their scientific activities published in reputable scientific journals. The Faculty aims to advance the educational and professional development of the students; generate and translate scientific findings into meaningful innovations in healthcare, and positively impact the health and well-being of the community we serve and beyond.

The Faculty awarded a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy in addition to a master's degree in pharmaceutical sciences, and clinical nutrition.
